The Artist

Theresa Coggiola

Master Hair Artist & Visual Artist / Painter

Master hair artist and working painter. Seventeen years of craft — trained in Chicago at Capri College, refined at Mario Tricoci and The Ruby Room. Color theory rooted in abstract expressionism: complex correction and luxury blonding treated as fine-art translation, not chemical service.

“Sacred dances” — how light moves through pigment, how tones bleed and shift at the edges. Your hair is not a standard timed session slot; it is the ultimate canvas.

Beauty alchemy truly is a nervous system’s best friend. Come as you are and relax — it’s a feel-good space. One artist. One chair. A private studio inside Ashes & Steel, downtown Asheville.
